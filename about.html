Full Stack Development Immersive is designed to take individuals with little to no programming experience and make them successful software developers. The program consists of 16, 1-unit semester equivalent courses and 4, 2-unit semester equivalent courses for a total program length of 30 weeks. The student workload for this program is a minimum of 1080 hours, where 360 hours is dedicated to contact work or instruction and 720 hours or more of outside study or preparation.

The term “Full Stack” means that students will be working with every layer in the “stack” of a typical web application: user interface, front end (browser), tier, and back end (server and database). The program has a deep emphasis and Python.
